200K - The Powerhouse Team

200K is one of two new LanceBotics general club teams. The team consists of many members from all grades, who are originally from teams 200H and 200X. The team hopes to compete and qualify at the 2021 VEX World Championship!



Retired Team 200X Achievements


  • Triple-Qualifier at the 2019 Toronto VEX Competition, with three awards received, all qualifying for the 2020 Ontario VRC Provincial Championship

  • A founding team of The 6 Alliance, an alliance of teams from all over the world

  • Created the Ontario VEX Discord Server to allow teams from all over Ontario to share ideas

  • Two-time Tournament Champions in Tower Takeover

  • Two Excellence Awards won during the Tower Takeover season

  • Two Judges Awards won in the 2019-2020 season

  • 39th in the world in Robot Skills in Tower Takeover, the highest ranked LanceBotics team

  • Qualified for the VEX World Championship two years in a row (2019 and 2020)

  • Elimination Round contender at every Regional and Provincial Competition

  • A total of 8 awards won in 2019-2020 Tower Takeover Season

  • Think Award winner at the 2019 Ontario VRC Provincial Championship
